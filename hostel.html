BELOW ARE SOME OF THE ROOM TYPES OFFERED BY OUR PARTNERED HOSTELS.

All room types are equally as good. Your preference is what matters.



Single Room


A single room is a room intended for one person to stay in. Therefore it has one bed and occupies only one person.




Double Room


A double room is a room intended for two people. A double room has double beds or a double decor. Some hostels refer to it as a twin room.




Quadruple Room


A quadruple room is a room that occupies four people. It is a four beddedroom which may have two double beds or four single beds.
